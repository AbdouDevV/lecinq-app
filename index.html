Le Cinq Restaurant App

About the App

Le Cinq Restaurant App is a mobile application that allows customers to browse the menu and order food online from Le Cinq Restaurant, located in Algeria.

Available on Android and iOS.

What the App Does

  • Browse the full restaurant menu
  • Place food orders online
  • Sign in securely using your Google account
  • Track your order history

Why We Request Google Account Access

We use Google Sign-In solely to authenticate users and create a personalized account. We access only your basic profile information (name and email) to identify your account. We do not share your data with third parties.
